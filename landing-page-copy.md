## FAQ

### 1. Is Topify another SEO dashboard?
No. Topify is built for **GEO**: measuring how AI engines such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview mention, rank, and cite your brand. It complements SEO reporting instead of replacing it.

### 2. Which teams usually own Topify internally?
The best buyer is usually a VP or director across product marketing, growth, SEO, or demand generation. Enterprise rollouts work best when content and analytics teams share the same AI visibility baseline.

### 3. How is this different from manually testing prompts?
Manual checks are inconsistent, hard to scale, and impossible to trend over time. Topify standardizes prompts, tracks changes daily, and ties every recommendation back to visibility, sentiment, position, and citation data.

### 4. What does the AI Agent actually do?
It audits your site, identifies prompt gaps, drafts content built for AI citation, and prepares publishing actions. Your team reviews every step before anything goes live.

### 5. Does Topify work for large SaaS buying journeys?
Yes. The product is strongest when buyers research categories, alternatives, integrations, and use cases across multiple stakeholders. That makes prompt-level visibility especially valuable for enterprise SaaS teams.

### 6. How quickly can a team see value?
Teams usually get immediate clarity on where competitors appear first in AI answers, then use that data to prioritize comparison pages, FAQs, and authority content.
